Our Story

UDGAM is SRMIST's premier sports festival, bringing together talented athletes and sports enthusiasts from across the nation. Since its inception, UDGAM has been a platform for showcasing athletic excellence, fostering team spirit, and building lasting friendships.

Each year, we strive to create an unforgettable experience that combines competitive sports, cultural events, and community building. Our festival has grown from a small campus event to one of the most anticipated sports gatherings in the collegiate circuit.
